Frequently Asked Questions

1.

How does TabForest make money?

TabForest generates revenue through non-intrusive advertisements displayed in new browser tabs. A significant portion (at least 50%) of this advertising revenue goes directly toward our tree-planting initiatives. This sustainable model allows us to keep the extension completely free for users while making a positive environmental impact.

2.

How are trees planted?

We partner with certified reforestation organizations like One Tree Planted and Arbor Day Foundation. Trees are planted in areas affected by deforestation, typically during planting seasons to ensure maximum survival rates. Each month, we allocate funds based on user activity and sponsor matching programs to plant thousands of trees worldwide.

3.

Who verifies the impact?

Our tree-planting partners provide regular reports including GPS coordinates of planting sites, species planted, and survival rates. We also work with independent auditors to verify these reports. Every quarter, we publish a transparency report on our website showing exactly how many trees were planted and where.

4.

Do I have to click ads?

No, you never need to click ads to support our mission. We earn revenue simply by displaying sponsored content in your new tab page. The ads are carefully curated to be relevant and unobtrusive. You can browse normally while still contributing to reforestation efforts with each new tab you open.

5.

Is my data safe?

Your privacy is our top priority. We do not collect personal browsing history or sell your data to third parties. All analytics are anonymized and used solely to improve the extension and report impact statistics. For more details, see our privacy policy.
